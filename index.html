What is a Travel Demand Forecast?

A travel demand forecast is like a crystal ball for transportation planning—but based on data, not magic. It predicts how people will move around in the future by looking at where we'll live, work, and play, and what travel options we'll have.

As the DFW region grows, millions more trips will happen every day. Will roads handle the traffic? Will public transit keep up? How can biking and walking fit in? A forecast helps us answer these questions by modeling future travel patterns under different scenarios-so we can plan smarter, invest wisely, and keep the region moving.
